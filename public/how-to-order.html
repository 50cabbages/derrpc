How to Order

1. Order via Delivery

To place an order for delivery:

  1. Save the receipt of your order from your device after clicking the "Confirm Order" button.
  2. Send the saved receipt to our Facebook page at DRE Computer Center or contact us at 09xxxxxxxx.
  3. Let us know that you'd like to place an order for delivery.
  4. We will instruct you to send the payment through GCash or bank transfer at 09xxxxxxxx.
  5. Upon payment confirmation, we will pack your order and have it delivered to your address.

2. Order for In-Store Pickup

To place an order for in-store pickup:

  1. Save the receipt of your order from your device after clicking the "Confirm Order" button.
  2. Send the saved receipt to our Facebook page at www.facebook.com or contact us at 09xxxxxxxx.
  3. Inform us that you'd like to pick up your order in-store.
  4. We will prepare your order and notify you once it is ready for pickup.
